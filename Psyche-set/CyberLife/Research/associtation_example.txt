WHY THIS FILE EXISTS
====================

Purpose: To help AI understand HOW MY BRAIN associates concepts

Goal: Train AI to predict MY specific associations, not generic ones

Example:
  Generic AI: "Cup" → "Drink" (obvious, everyone thinks this)
  My associations: "Cup" → "2 cups of flour" → "Baking" → "Cookies" →
                   "Mom's cookies" → "Childhood" → "Home" → "Safe"

The AI needs to learn:
1. Common associations (cup → drink)
2. Personal associations (cup → mom's cookies → childhood memory)
3. Emotional weights (childhood memory = 0.9 importance, strong warmth)
4. Chain depth (willing to go 6+ steps for meaningful connections)
5. Context switching (cup in kitchen vs cup in math vs trophy cup)
